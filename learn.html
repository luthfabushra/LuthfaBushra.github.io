Guide

Blood sugar basics (gentle version)

A starter guide you can expand over time.

First: glucose is not your enemy. It’s fuel. The goal isn’t “low.” The goal is steady enough for your life.

A simple framework

  • Inputs: what you eat, how you sleep, how you move, your stress load
  • Signals: hunger, energy, cravings, mood, focus
  • Patterns: what repeats (not what happens once)

Practical starting points

  • Protein and fiber at meals (not as rules, as support)
  • Movement you can repeat (walking counts)
  • Earlier sleep when possible (even 20 minutes helps)

Gentle education means you leave with clarity — not shame.
