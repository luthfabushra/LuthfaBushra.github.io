The most underrated health skill: recovery

Rest isn’t weakness. It’s a physiological process you can support — on purpose.

Science, simply — Oct 14, 2025 — Fenotypefairy

Recovery isn’t only for athletes. It’s for anyone living in a body — especially one carrying stress.

Think of recovery as signals of safety: warmth, nourishment, rest, steady breath, and social softness.

Recovery is not laziness. It’s biology. And it’s something you can support gently, consistently, and without turning it into another performance.

A few signals of safety

  • Regular meals (not perfection)
  • Earlier nights when possible
  • Gentle movement that feels like relief
  • Warmth, sunlight, and a steadier breath
